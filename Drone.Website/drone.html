Our Story

Our story begins in late fall of 2014, where our team at Iowa BIG, an initiative based school located in Cedar Rapids Iowa. We envisioned a revolutionary way of surveying farmland using aerial technology. We saw an opportunity to take images, and run them through our self developed code, with which we will be able to better analyze what areas of the farm need help. But our innovative team didn't stop there. We are currently working on using Thermal imaging technology to analyze energy efficiency in buildings across the corridor. As we grow as a team, we hope to develop new technology and ideas that allows Unmanned Aerial Systems to benefit society.
