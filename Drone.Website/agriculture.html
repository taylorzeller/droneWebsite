Agriculture

Crops serve as an important part of the Iowa Economy, producing the food that the world thrives on. However, we believe that our technology, can help increase efficiency in the Agriculture industry, leaving a lasting on the world. This belief inspired our first project, which is built on the hypothesis that by using an aerial image, and using a code that our team developed, we can find what crops need attention. We use this informations to help farmers do what they do best, and farm. This will save farmers time, and in the long term, make them money.
